Music

Ever since I was little I've made and played music. I first taught myself how to play piano and then I learned Guitar. Since then, I've learned a lot more instruments and gotten a lot better at playing them. I make sure that I play an instrument every single day. Doing that will help me progress at all the instruments I know, even if it's for a short amount of time. Not only do I play music, but I also make it. Recording is a total different hobby that I enjoy just as much as playing. When I first started playing, I played a lot of classic rock, rock, and metal. Once I learned how to make and record music, I've put out a lot of instrumentals (music with no words). Instrumentals are very interesting to me. They have to be interesting and unique all without any lyrics. It's a lot harder getting someone to enjoy a song that is only music, no singing. I studied and practiced making songs very creative and became obsessed with instrumentals. I love all kinds of music though, and I create anything that is on my mind. My music is me.
